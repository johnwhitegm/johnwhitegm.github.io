While browsing the Wayback Machine for Classic WoW content, I stumbled across a "World of Warcraft Fan Site Kit" which was released in early 2004, many months before the official North American launch on November 23, 2004. There's also a WotLK version from 2007!

Inspired by this discovery, I set out to build a website entirely from that very kit. The site you see here is drawn directly from those original assets of logos, artwork, screenshots, and templates. I have thoughtfully organized all the assets as a tribute to that era. This project is both a nostalgic homage and a preservation of the World of Warcraft Fan Site Kit itself. I hope you enjoy exploring it as much as I enjoyed bringing it to life.

For reference, Blizzard announced the World of Warcraft Fan Site Kit as the following:
World of Warcraft Fan Site Kit [21.2 MB â€“ ZIP]
The World of Warcraft Fan Site Kit is a package of materials designed to assist in the creation of your own WoW Fan Site. Specifically, the kit includes logos, hi-res artwork, screenshots, graphic PSD templates, a simple example of an HTML page, and a host of character cut-outs sorted by class. We hope you'll find this kit useful, and we look forward to seeing what you've created!
